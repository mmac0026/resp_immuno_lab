Respiratory Immunology Lab

Research Overview

Our laboratory, part of Monash Immunology and based at the Alfred Hospital, is led by Professor Ben Marsland. Our main research focus revolves around the microbiome in the gut, lung, and skin, and how it can influence respiratory diseases. In particular, we study host-microbe interactions within the context of allergy, asthma, cystic fibrosis, and lung transplantation.
